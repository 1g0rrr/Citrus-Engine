A Platform is a rectangular object that is meant to be stood on. It can be given any position, width, height, or rotation to suit your level's needs. You can make your platform a "one-way" or "cloud" platform so that you can jump on from underneath (collision is only applied when coming from above it). There are two ways of adding graphics for your platform. You can give your platform a graphic just like you would any other object (by passing a graphical class into the view property) or you can leave your platform invisible and line it up with your backgrounds for a more custom look. Properties: oneWay - Makes the platform only collidable when falling from above it.
